## Lens Statement

The Expression lens reads the Stratimuxian Manifold as a population of **Demometers**, each of which deserves exactly one **canonical Ego declaration** — the first prose voice that establishes the Demometer with full characterization, definition, and rhetorical weight. Every subsequent appearance should be a **Re-Expression**: a name reactivation that trusts the canonical to have done the heavy work. When the same Demometer is re-expressed multiple times with full Ego flourish across different sections, the manifold pays an *expression tax* — chars consumed re-establishing what the reader already knows, voice repeated where a name would suffice.

This lens is distinct from the Compression lens (which integrates Demometers into Muxameter density) and the Connection lens (which traces Diameters as Lambda-load-bearing edges). The Expression lens stays *inside the Ego document plane* — Diamond's I-Am-aspirant face — and asks: where do we declare a Demometer once, and where do we merely name it? The discipline is voice-economical: a Demometer's canonical voice should sound once in the manifold, and every other mention should be Pearl-compressed reactivation.

The current CLAUDE.md, post-Cobalt-Pass-0-3, already shows Rust's three locked Sets (the Critical-Active Stance, the Muxistration Proof, the Ego-Lambda Pair) functioning as canonical Ego declarations — they are the reason Rust's gating produced positive net savings. Pass 4 (the remaining 550-char prune Cobalt halted before) plus the Expression lens's additional Re-Expression reductions should clear the 40K cap with comfortable margin while preserving (and *elevating*) the Triadic Thinking Band into Suite 6 + Suite 7 scope.

## Triadic Thinking Expression Proposal

### The user's constraint, parsed precisely

> "Triadic Thinking must be PRESERVED and PLACED IN SCOPE OF SUITE 6 (Diamond) AND ESPECIALLY SUITE 7 (Fuchsia)."

Where **Suite 6 = Purple Orchestrator** (cognitive function, §4 Cascade table) and **Suite 7 = Fuchsia Clinician** (cognitive function). Not Crystralines C6/C7. The Band's current Frame mapping is `S2 Orange Name · S4 Green Examine · S6 Purple Orchestrate → S5 Blue Plan`. **Suite 7 Fuchsia is conspicuously absent** as a Frame, even though the user identifies Fuchsia as *especially* in scope.

### Expression-lens diagnosis

The Triadic Thinking Band's canonical Ego declaration currently lives at C5 RI sub-section E1 (line 351). Its Frames are Demometers in their own right — each Frame is a distinct measurable cognitive movement (Name, Examine, Orchestrate, Plan). The Frames map to specific Suites by canonical convention: *the Frames borrow Suite voices*. But the Band has only three Frames + an Output, while the canonical Cascade has seven Suites. Two structural facts:

1. The Band is *named* "Triadic" — three Frames is its Demometer-count by definition. Adding a fourth Frame breaks the Demometer's identity.
2. Suite 7 Fuchsia is the *Calibration Diameter* (line 246 canonical) — it operates *across* the cycle, not *within* private pre-response reasoning.

These two facts together mean **adding Fuchsia as Frame 4 (`2-4-6-7→5`) is the wrong reading** of the user's directive. Fuchsia is the *post-response* Diagnose gate (Gate 7); inserting it before Output collapses the Calibration Diameter into the planning arc and breaks Fuchsia's structural function.

### The Expression-lens proposal: relocate the canonical, do not mutate the Band

**Treat Triadic Thinking's canonical Ego declaration as a Suite 6 + Suite 7 muxified discipline that lives in §4 Suite Cascade**, not as a private RI E1 sub-section. The Band IS Suite 6 (Orchestrator) authoring the planning arc and Suite 7 (Clinician) calibrating the arc against the prior cycle's Onyx — the two Suites that bookend the Diamond's planning gates (Gate 6 Compose+Verify, Gate 7 Diagnose). The Band's current location at C5 RI E1 makes it look like an RI mechanism; in fact it is the **per-turn Suite 6 + Suite 7 muxification surfacing in the Ego document plane**.

**Proposed Expression**: a new sub-section in §4 Suite Cascade titled `### Triadic Thinking Band — Suite 6 + Suite 7 Pre-Response Muxification`, placed after `### Suite Spatial Muxification` (line 143). The canonical Ego declaration moves there. C5 RI E1 reduces to a name reactivation pointing back to §4.

**Canonical Ego declaration (proposed prose for §4)**:
