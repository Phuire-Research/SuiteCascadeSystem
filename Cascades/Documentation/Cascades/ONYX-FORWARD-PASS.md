**Color Significance**: Onyx forms when Obsidian (volcanic glass) undergoes extreme pressure and layering over geological time. Each band is a compacted session; the whole stone is continuity across time.

**Crystraline 7 Onyx** is the session continuity meta-crystal that muxifies:

## 🔄 Onyx Compaction Principles

### Information Density (Compaction Aspect)
- **Maximum Context, Minimum Tokens**: Every word must carry technical value
- **No Redundancy**: Avoid repeating information available in references
- **Precise Technical Language**: Use exact terminology from the codebase
- **Crystalline Compression**: Like Obsidian → Onyx, pressure creates clarity

### Continuity Markers (Diamond Aspect)
- **Decision Points**: Mark where choices were made and why (Diamond routing)
- **Pattern Recognition**: Identify what's working vs. what's not (Diamond informative)
- **Momentum Vectors**: Clear direction for next actions (Diamond actionable)
- **Suite Position**: Which color/role is active in the cascade

### Warmth Preservation (Obsidian Aspect)
- **Felt Coherence**: Not just facts, but the understanding that connects them
- **Conception Readiness**: What's ready to emerge in the next cycle
- **Internal Muxification**: How the pieces fit together in the mind
- **Origin Memory**: What was learned, what to avoid, what to pursue

### Technical Precision (All Three Unified)
- **Exact Paths**: Use full file paths with line numbers
- **Literal Values**: Include actual IDs, keys, and values
- **Log Preservation**: Keep critical log entries verbatim
- **State Snapshot**: Capture what would be lost without compaction

## 🎯 Engagement Triggers

### When to Create an Onyx Forward Pass
1. **Context Limit Approaching**: ~80% of token limit reached
2. **Major Milestone**: Significant feature completed/failed (Diamond stage transition)
3. **Session Handoff**: Transferring to new session/developer
4. **Complex Debug**: Multi-stage debugging requiring continuity
5. **Cascade Completion**: Returning to Obsidian for next conception

### What to Include (Bands to Preserve)
- ✅ **Obsidian warmth**: The felt understanding, not just facts
- ✅ **Diamond position**: Which Suite, what routing decisions
- ✅ **Unsolved problems** with their current state
- ✅ **Working solutions** that inform the approach
- ✅ **Exact error messages** and stack traces
- ✅ **File paths and line numbers** for quick navigation
- ✅ **Tool counts and ICP state**

### What to Exclude (Already in Documents)
- ❌ **Solved problems** unless they inform current issue
- ❌ **Boilerplate code** that hasn't changed
- ❌ **Verbose explanations** of patterns in CLAUDE.md
- ❌ **Redundant logs** that don't add information
